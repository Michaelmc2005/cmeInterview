I became interested in Virtual Reality development and showcased my 3d modelling abilities which I taught myself over the past 4 years at the Whitla Hall, Queen's University, and this lead me to win the Sentinus Young Innovators 2022 competition in October, This achievement entitles me to be the only person to represent Northern Ireland, at a global event in Dallas, Texas in May this year.
